Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to estimate how many portable restrooms I need for an event?

For portable restrooms for events, a general rule is one unit per 50 guests for a 4-hour event. For longer events or those serving alcohol, increase to one per 40 guests. We provide detailed planning assistance.

What is the biggest factor affecting portable toilet rental prices?

The single biggest factor in portable restroom rental cost is usually the unit type. A standard unit is the most economical, while luxury restroom trailers are a premium investment due to their features and size.

Do luxury portable toilets require electricity and water?

Yes, most luxury portable toilets and all restroom trailers require a standard 110v electrical outlet for lighting and climate control, and a water hose connection for sink and toilet operation.

What typically makes a porta potty 'fancy'?

A fancy porta potty rental often includes upgrades like a more durable and attractive exterior, improved interior lighting and ventilation, a hand sanitizer dispenser, and sometimes a small shelf or mirror.

Should I choose a local or national portable toilet company?

A national portable toilet business with local service hubs, like Us, offers the advantage of large-scale resources and standardized quality, combined with local delivery and service teams for responsiveness.

New York City is at the southern tip of New York State and is situated on one of the world's largest natural harbors. The city comprises five boroughs, each of which is coextensive with a respective county. The five boroughs, which were created in 1898 when local governments were consolidated into a single municipality, are: Brooklyn (Kings County), Queens (Queens County), Manhattan (New York County), the Bronx (Bronx County), and Staten Island (Richmond County). New York City is a global city and a cultural, financial, high-tech, entertainment, glamour, and media center with a significant influence on commerce, health care and scientific output in life sciences, research, technology, education, politics, tourism, dining, art, fashion, and sports. Home to the headquarters of the United Nations, New York is an important center for international diplomacy, and it is sometimes described as the world's most important city and the capital of the world.
